My Projects

Pontiac G6

My Pontiac G6, obviously you've seen a lot of this car. This has been my project for quite some time. I have done a lot of maintainance from changing oil to changing the fueling system. I have replaced fuel injectors, the fuel pump, and some electrical stuff along with that. Not only maintaining the vehicle, I have also modified it. I have a custom 3inch exhaust out the back with a single resonator. So, it is loud. Not only that, but the car is lowered on some Eibach springs. I am hoping to get some coilovers for it eventually. However, these work for now. I have changed some interior pieces, like the radio with an aftermarket one, and done some extensive cleaning.

Honda Accord

This was a project I worked on to flip, with the Pontiac being my daily driver. It is a 1993 Honda Accord with over 300k miles on the clock! That's a lot, if you ask me. For a 1993 Honda, however, it seemed alright. It had many issues, but the main piece was that it had an intact interior. I replaced the fuel pump assembly, upgraded the pump, and put in an aftermarket filter. I cleaned a LOT of the upper intake. I spent a lot of time working on this car, bought it for about $350 from a guy on Facebook Marketplace. Then managed to sell the car for $1800. Overall, I made a profit of roughly $1000. For the amount of time spent on the car, I would say it was worth it.
